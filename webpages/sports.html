Indian womens cricket team heartbreak loss
against australia in worldcup semifinal


India crashed to a defeat vs Australia in their semi-final fixture of the ongoing Women's
T20 World Cup 2023, at the Newlands Cricket Ground in Cape Town, on Thursday.
Chasing a target of 173 runs, India reached 167/8 in 20 overs, with Harmanpreet Kaur's
half-century going in vain, losing by five runs. The India captain slammed 52 runs off
34 balls, including six fours and a maximum. For Australia's bowling department,
Darcie Brown and Ashleigh Gardner were in good form and took two wickets each
respectively. Initially, Beth Mooney was in brilliant form and grabbed a half-century as
Australia posted 172/4 in 20 overs. Mooney slammed 54 runs off 37 balls, packed with
seven fours and a six. Meanwhile, captain Meg Lanning smacked an unbeaten knock of
49 runs off 34 balls, including four fours and two sixes. Shikha Pandey was in good
bowling form for India and took two wickets, Deepti Sharma and Radha Yadav bagged
a dismissal each.
